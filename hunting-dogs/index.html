GPS Tracker for Hunting Dogs Without Cell Service

Track Your Hunting Dog — Even Where There Is No Signal

When hunting dogs run deep into forests, mountains, or rural fields, cell coverage disappears. Most GPS trackers stop working the moment they lose the GSM network.

Loko GPS Tracker is different.

Loko tracks your dog without cellular networks, SIM cards, or internet. It uses long-range LoRa radio communication to send GPS location directly to your phone. This means you can track your dog even in the most remote hunting areas.

How Loko Works

Loko uses a simple two-device system:

1

Loko Air (attached to the dog)

A tiny GPS transmitter that sends location data using LoRa radio.

2

Loko Ground (carried by the hunter)

A receiver that collects the location data and sends it to your smartphone via Bluetooth.

Your phone displays the dog's location using the offline map inside the Loko app.

No internet required.
