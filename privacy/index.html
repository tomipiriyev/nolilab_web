Privacy Policy for Loko App

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Zero Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any user data. The Loko app operates by displaying location on maps and receiving Bluetooth data from the ground unit only.

Information Collection and Use

The Loko application requires specific device permissions to function properly:

Bluetooth Permission

The application needs Bluetooth enabled to receive location information from the Loko Ground unit via wireless connectivity. This permission allows the app to:

  • Connect to your Loko Ground receiver device
  • Receive location data transmitted from the Loko Air tracker
  • Maintain communication between your phone and the ground unit

Location Permission

Device location access allows the app to display your position on maps and show your relationship to the Loko Air unit. This permission is used to:

  • Show your current position on the map interface
  • Calculate distance between you and the tracked device
  • Provide accurate mapping and navigation features

Data Handling Commitment

Important: We do not collect, store, or share any user data. The app only displays location on maps and receives Bluetooth data from the ground unit. All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to Nolilab servers or any third parties.

Children's Privacy

The Loko App is appropriate for users of all ages, including children. It only displays location data on maps and does not contain any inappropriate content.

Since the app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, there are no privacy concerns related to children's use of the application. All location information remains local to the user's device.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: akio@nolilab.com
  • Live Chat: Available in the bottom left corner of our website at www.nolilab.com

Privacy Summary

The Loko App is built with privacy in mind:

  • ✅ No data collection - We don't collect any user information
  • ✅ No cloud storage - All data stays on your device
  • ✅ No third-party sharing - Your data is never shared
  • ✅ Local operation only - Works entirely on your device
  • ✅ Bluetooth communication - Direct device-to-device only
  • ✅ Safe for all ages - Appropriate content for everyone

By using the Loko App, you can be confident that your privacy is protected. We never collect, store, or share your location data or personal information.
