Loko GPS Tracker Firmware Updates & Downloads

Download the latest firmware for your Loko GPS tracker. Free updates for LoRa P2P and LoRaWAN modes. We recommend keeping your devices on the latest firmware. Contact us anytime for support.

To Upgrade Loko Air Firmware

Loko Air Firmware Upgrade Tutorial

1
Download Loko Air firmware Download
2
Download STMCubeProgrammer Download
3
Open STM32CubeProgrammer
4
Connect Loko Air to PC via USB
5
On right side select UART
6
Select correct port on USB devices
7
Parity select "None"
8
Hit "Connect" button and wait to read Loko binary
9
Once it is connected to Loko microcontroller, hit "Erasing & Programming" section on left side bar
10
Only check "Run after programming"
11
Select file path of the new firmware
12
Hit "Start Programming"
13
Wait until upload is complete
14
Unplug the Loko Air unit, then turn off and on again to reset the device
15
Ready to use
