Enhance Your Outdoor Adventures with noliLab's Loko GPS Tracker

Overview

GPS technology has become essential for outdoor navigation, transforming how we explore trails, navigate backcountry, and enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, biking, and drone operations. The Loko GPS Tracker from noliLab provides reliable tracking capabilities without the need for SIM cards or monthly subscription fees, making it the perfect companion for your outdoor adventures.

Key Device Features

Ultra-Lightweight and Waterproof

The Loko tracker weighs merely 12 grams with IPX7 water-resistance rating and crash protection. This minimal weight means you won't even notice it attached to your gear, while the waterproof construction ensures reliable operation even in heavy rain or when accidentally submerged.

Exceptional Battery Performance

Battery performance is remarkable: "The Air unit (transmitter) can last up to 250 days per charge" depending on configuration and transmission intervals. This extended runtime eliminates the constant recharging required by smartphones and other tracking devices, letting you focus on your adventure rather than battery management.

Extended Communication Range

The device offers 5+ kilometer range using LoRa technology, far exceeding Bluetooth-based alternatives. This extended range means you can monitor equipment or travel companions even when separated by significant distances in outdoor environments.

Positioning Accuracy

With approximately 2.5-meter GPS accuracy under good conditions, the Loko tracker provides precise location data for navigation and recovery operations. This accuracy level proves sufficient for virtually all outdoor adventure scenarios.

System Architecture

The Loko system comprises two complementary components working together:

Ground Unit (Receiver)

The Ground Unit serves as your base station, supporting up to 30 Air Units simultaneously. It connects to smartphones via Bluetooth, displaying location data through the companion app. This architecture enables monitoring multiple pieces of equipment or tracking several group members from a single device.

Air Unit (Transmitter)

The compact Air Unit attaches to whatever you want to trackâ€”backpacks, bikes, drones, pets, or valuable equipment. It transmits position data via LoRa radio to the Ground Unit for relay to your smartphone.

Communication Technology

LoRa radio and Bluetooth connectivity work together to deliver reliable tracking. LoRa provides the long-range communication between Air and Ground units, while Bluetooth connects the Ground unit to your smartphone for data visualization and control.

Practical Applications for Outdoor Enthusiasts

Pet Safety Monitoring

Attach the tracker to pet collars for safety monitoring during outdoor adventures. The extended battery life means weeks of operation without recharging, while water resistance ensures functionality even when pets swim or play in wet conditions.

Drone Location Tracking

Monitor drone positions during flights and quickly locate crashed or lost aircraft. At just 12 grams, the tracker doesn't significantly impact flight performance while providing invaluable recovery capabilities when things go wrong.

Belongings Protection

Track valuable equipment and belongings during outdoor adventures. Attach trackers to backpacks, camping gear, or bikes to prevent loss and enable quick recovery if items go missing.

IoT Device Integration

For advanced users, the Loko integrates with LoRaWAN networks, enabling integration into larger Internet of Things deployments for environmental monitoring, wildlife tracking, or distributed sensor networks.

Setup Instructions

Initial Charging

Charge both Air and Ground units via 5V USB Type-C connections before first use. Full charging typically takes a few hours and provides weeks or months of operation depending on configuration.

Smartphone Connection

Connect the Ground Unit to smartphones via Bluetooth pairing. The process is straightforward, requiring only a few taps to establish the connection.

App Configuration

Download and configure the companion app, available for both iOS and Android platforms. The intuitive interface provides mapping, tracking history, and configuration options for customizing update intervals and alert settings.
