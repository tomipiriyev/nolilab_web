How to Upgrade Loko Firmware

Keeping your Loko GPS tracker up to date ensures optimal performance and access to the latest features. This comprehensive guide walks you through the firmware upgrade process for both Loko Air and Loko Ground units.

Upgrading Loko Air Firmware

Follow these 15 steps carefully to update your Loko Air device firmware:

  1. Obtain the Loko Air firmware file from the official nolilab website or GitHub repository
  2. Download and install STM32CubeProgrammer software on your computer
  3. Launch the STM32CubeProgrammer application
  4. Connect your Loko Air device to your computer using a USB cable
  5. In the right panel of the application, select UART as the connection method
  6. Identify and select the correct USB port from the dropdown menu
  7. Set the parity setting to "None"
  8. Click the "Connect" button and wait for the device binary to be read
  9. Navigate to the "Erasing & Programming" section in the left menu
  10. Enable only the "Run after programming" checkbox
  11. Click the browse button to specify the path to your new firmware file
  12. Initiate the programming sequence by clicking the start button
  13. Wait for the upload process to complete fully - do not disconnect during this process
  14. Once complete, disconnect the unit and power cycle the device
  15. Your Loko Air device is now ready for operation with the updated firmware

Upgrading Loko Ground Firmware

The firmware update process for the Loko Ground unit requires different software:

  1. Download and install the Thonny IDE application on your computer
  2. Download the latest Loko Ground firmware package from the official repository
  3. Connect your Loko Ground Unit to your computer via USB cable
  4. Activate the device by pressing the physical power button
  5. Open the Thonny IDE application
  6. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the firmware update process

Important Notes

  • Always ensure your device is fully charged before starting the firmware update process
  • Do not disconnect the device during the firmware upload - this could brick your device
  • Keep a backup of your current configuration settings before upgrading
  • If you encounter any issues, consult the official documentation or contact nolilab support

Conclusion

Regular firmware updates help maintain optimal performance and security for your Loko GPS tracking system. By following these step-by-step instructions, you can safely upgrade both the Air and Ground units to the latest firmware versions.
